Ballistics Reforged is a rebuilt ballistics and battlefield-feel mod for Factorio 2.0.

It focuses on making bullets, rockets, shells, shotgun pellets, turret fire, and impacts feel more physical while keeping the game readable in large fights.

Features:
- Physical bullet tracers
- Subtle / Balanced / Cinematic FX presets
- Impact layering and big-fight FX cap
- Tuned shotgun behaviour
- Physical rockets with smoke and heavier impact feel
- Arcing shells and throwables
- Turret projectile leading
- Optional wall-blocked spitter projectiles
- Cleaner impact smoke, dust, debris, and bug-hit feedback

1.0.0 is the first stable public release.
